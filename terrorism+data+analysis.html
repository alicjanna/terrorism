How many perpetrators do have suicidal tendencies?¶

It is widely known, that terrorists don't care about people's lives... neither about theirs. How many of them do have suicidal tendencies? What is the % distribution of deaths excluding those caused by explosions and bombings?

#extract data with suicidal perpetrators

One can be surprised by number of attacks executed with fake weapons. The analysis says that more than a half of these attacks was successful (whatever it means).

#extract data with fake weapon

How safe is abortion?¶

One of columns in the database described the target of the attack. There was a special category for abortion related actions. It was also divided into violence targeted against clinics and personnel. Apparently, that kind of violence still takes place.

In [6]:
#question 4 - How safe is abortion?
#explanation - Distribution of attacks connected with abortion (clinics and personnel).

#data retrieval
abort = data.loc[data['targtype1_txt'] == 'Abortion Related']

clin = abort.loc[abort['targsubtype1_txt'] == 'Clinics']
per = abort.loc[abort['targsubtype1_txt'] == 'Personnel']

#calculate attacks per year and divide by target
year_list = sorted(np.unique(data['iyear']))
typeat_series = {}
for typeat in np.unique(abort['targsubtype1_txt']):
    series = []
    typeat_idx = data['targsubtype1_txt'] == typeat
    for year in year_list:
        series.append((abort.loc[(data['iyear'] == year) & typeat_idx].shape[0]))
    typeat_series[typeat] = series

#plot
fig = plt.figure(figsize=(10,10))
for typeat, series in typeat_series.items():
    plt.plot(year_list,series, label=typeat)
fig.suptitle('abortion related attacks over time', fontsize = 16)
plt.legend(bbox_to_anchor=(1.05, 1), loc=2, borderaxespad=0.)
plt.xlabel('year')
plt.ylabel('number of attacks')
plt.show()

How does weapon type affect number of victims?¶

Conducted analysis proved some obvious facts:

  • bombings/explosions are most dangerous
  • firearms attacks leave more fatalities than injuries. In addition, it was discovered (re-discovered or just proved) that attacks using vehicles or chemical weapons are less deadly than betore mentioned types.
